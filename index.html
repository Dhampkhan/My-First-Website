Exotic Cats

The Exotic is a breed of cat developed as a short-haired version of the Persian. The Exotic is similar to the Persian in many ways, including temperament, a flat nose & face with the exceptions of the short dense coat. The Exotic breed generally lives 12-15 years of age.

Cat Grooming

Many long-haired cats need grooming every day to prevent knots from forming, while short-haired cats generally do a good job of grooming themselves. However, grooming sessions at least once a week are still important to help keep their coat in a good condition.
